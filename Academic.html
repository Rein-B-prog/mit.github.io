Machakos Institute of Technology Courses

MIT offers various courses in electrical, engineering, hospitality, masonry, ICT, and more.

MIT offers a diverse range of programs across different schools:

School of Social Work and Community Development

Prepares students for careers in social development, welfare, and community service.

School of Business Education

Programs in business management, project planning, and human resources.

School of Applied and Health Sciences

Focuses on health sector careers, including nutrition and community health.

School of Computing and Information Technology

Courses in information technology and digital skills.

School of Hospitality Management

Practical skills for hospitality and tourism industry careers.

School of Accounts and Finance

Training in accounting and finance for professional certifications.

School of Hairdressing and Beauty Therapy

Courses in hairdressing and beauty therapy.

School of Engineering

Programs in electrical and electronics engineering, plumbing, and more.

The institute also offers diploma and certificate courses. Fees vary by course level and mode of study. For more details, visit the official MIT website or contact the admissions office.
