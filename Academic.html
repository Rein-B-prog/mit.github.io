Machakos Institute of Technology Courses

Machakos Institute of Technology (MIT) offers a diverse range of courses
across various schools, catering to different career paths.
Here are some of the courses available at MIT:

School of Social Work and Community Development:

Prepares students for careers in social development, welfare, and community service.

School of Business Education:

Offers programs in business management, project planning, and human resources.

School of Applied and Health Sciences:

Focuses on health sector careers, including nutrition and community health.

School of Computing and Information Technology:

Provides courses in information technology and digital skills.

School of Hospitality Management:

Equips students with practical skills for the hospitality and tourism industry.

School of Accounts and Finance:

Offers training in accounting and finance, preparing students for professional certifications.

School of Hairdressing and Beauty Therapy:

Focuses on the beauty industry, offering courses in hairdressing and beauty therapy.

School of Engineering:

Provides programs in electrical and electronics engineering, plumbing, and more.

The institute also offers diploma and certificate courses
with fees varying based on the course level and mode of study.
For more detailed information on courses, fees, and admission requirements
you can visit the official MIT website or contact their admissions office.
