ADMISSION PROCEDURES

(a) Apply online today by filling the COURSE APPLICATION FORM
or
(b) Download application form here MIT APPLICATION FORM

1. A student must apply for a course of study (Application letter).

2. The front office team will ascertain whether the student is qualified to pursue that course.

KNEC COURSES

For Diploma courses: minimum grade C- and above.
For Certificate courses: minimum grade D plain and above.
For Artisan Courses: minimum grade D-.

KASNEB COURSES

CPA: minimum grade C+
ATD: minimum grade C- and above
Certificate in Accountancy Management Skills (CAMS): minimum grade D+ and above.

3. If qualified, students will be asked to pay an admission fee and fill the admission form or submit the downloaded form with the admission fee:

  • Kshs 2,000 for long courses
  • Kshs 500 for short courses

This money is payable at the MIT bank accounts:

  • Equity Bank Ltd – Machakos Branch, A/C 0600294705830
  • Co-operatives Bank Ltd – Machakos Branch, A/C 01136039381600
  • All examination fees: Equity Bank Ltd – Machakos Branch, A/C: 0600298154392

Paybill no: 906650, Account No: Name of the student.

REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Copy of I.D / Waiting card / Baptismal card
  • Copies of academic certificates
  • Two coloured passport photos
