About Us

Machakos Institute of Technology is a TVET institution located along the Machakos–Nairobi highway at Kinyali House, 2nd floor.

The institution offers a wide range of technical and vocational courses designed to equip students with practical skills for the job market.

We pride ourselves on providing quality education, experienced instructors, and modern learning facilities to ensure student success.

Our Mission

To train students to practice at the highest level of professional competence.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in technical and vocational training.
