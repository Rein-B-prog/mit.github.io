Machakos Institute of Technology is a TVET school located alongside Machakos-Nairobi highway. The institution is located in Kinyali House second floor.

Machakos Institute of Technology offers various courses in the field of technology and engineering. The institution is known for its quality education and skilled instructors. Students at Machakos Institute of Technology have access to modern facilities and resources to enhance their learning experience.

Our school motto is:
Master the training and Experience the rewards

The institution is a registered technical training college focused on
providing quality and affordable education
to students in the region and practical education.

Our Core Values

  • Mission: To train students to practice at the highest level of professional competence.
  • Focus: Offering technical, vocational, and business courses (including KNEC courses).
  • Goal: Producing holistic individuals.
